About me

Who am I?

I am a 27 year old man from Norway. I started my journey as a PT/coach when i was around 23. i Started early cause i fell in love with training, and got a fast grip of it, which lead this way for me. Currently Im studying to be a physiotherapist in New Castle. In my spare time i like to play video games, watching series, and sometimes occasionally going out for a drink with friends. My life is no different from others i believe, so like others i have a pretty normal life.

These are both very fun games to play, and i recommend them strongly. If I'm not playing games on my PC, im Usually out walking or hanging out with friends. I am very interest in our body and it's anatomy, and that made me start out as a nurse to begin with, but i wanted to know more, so this is why i started my degree as an physiotherapist. These are my interest, which can for many seem like a odd combo, which i can understand because i have not met someone with the same combo of interests yet.

My past

As many people, I had a good childhood with good people around me, who supported me for who i was. Everything went well, except when i became a teenager where i started to slowly gain a food disorder. I got really skinny, and drastically lost weight. i was in and out at the hospital, because they had to force feed me. This is a dark part in my life, and something i just want to forget. I did not have any energy at all, so all i did was laying at home starving my self. I hated the way i looked, but i had no controll over my own body. When i was around 17 i started to get better because of better treatment, which saved my life. When i got into my twenties i was fully recoverd. This disorder has a lot to do with my career choices. I became a nurse to help other people like i was in the past, and later on became a PT and now a physiotherapist.

I hope this can inspired others to help each other or just look out for each other, because food disorder can really dig you into a dark scary hole.
